What are nicotine pouches?

Small, tobacco‑leaf‑free pouches that go under your lip. They deliver nicotine and flavor and are spit‑free and smokeless.

Why they’re different

Because there is no burning (combustion) and no aerosolization of e‑liquid, nicotine pouches don’t create smoke or vapor. That means no ash, tar, or second‑hand smoke.

What the research says

Azzopardi et al., 2021 (Drug and Chemical Toxicology) compared modern oral nicotine pouches with cigarettes using biomarkers of exposure. In adult participants who used pouches exclusively, levels of several smoke‑related toxicant biomarkers were substantially lower than in cigarette smokers, and in some cases close to levels seen in non‑smokers or users of nicotine replacement therapy.
